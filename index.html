WHAT IS TESTOSTERONE?

While most people have heard of testosterone, a lot don’t truly understand the intricacies of it. Testosterone is found in men and women and is a hormone. Men’s testosterone is produced in the testicles, whereas women’s testosterone is produced in the ovaries. Testosterone usually spikes around the age of puberty for men, but then starts to decline somewhere between the age of 30 and 40.
